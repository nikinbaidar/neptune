Neural Networks Mindset

Neural networks for me, when I was just starting out was very boring. This was partly because training models took a lot of time and, partly because I was in the wrong mindset. Neural networks are like modern day science experiments where nothing is certain. It is kinda like how scientists worked in the past. Scientists in the past ran tons of experiments to come to a specific conclusion about something. They tried bunch of stuff, that may or may not have made sense. This sort of workflow has pretty much banished in most fields of work. There is a strict protocol that one must follow to get what the achieve but this does not translate to neural networks.

I normally work with 2 panes open so this resembles my work flow.
